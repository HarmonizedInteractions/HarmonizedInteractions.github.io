Norihide Kitaoka
北岡 教英
CEO / Representative Director
Professor, Dept. of Computer Science and Engineering, Toyohashi University of Technology

After graduating from Kyoto University and working at DENSO Corporation, he joined Toyohashi University of Technology. He specializes in speech information processing, natural language processing, and multimodal dialogue systems, and is known for his research on elderly speech recognition and real-environment speech processing. 179 peer-reviewed papers and 581 presentations. Vice President of the IEICE Information and Systems Society, and Chair of the APSIPA Japan Chapter. Ph.D. in Engineering.

Akinobu Lee
李 晃伸
Director
Professor, Dept. of Computer Science, Nagoya Institute of Technology

Ph.D. in Informatics from Kyoto University. Previously at Nara Institute of Science and Technology. Developer of "Julius," a large vocabulary continuous speech recognition engine maintained for over 30 years. Also the creator of "MMDAgent," a speech interaction toolkit, and "MMDAgent-EX," an avatar-based voice interaction toolkit. Specializes in speech recognition, spoken dialogue systems, and avatar communication.
